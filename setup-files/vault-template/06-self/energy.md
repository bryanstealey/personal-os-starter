# Energy

Longitudinal patterns in your energy — when you are sharp, when you are depleted, what reliably recovers you, what reliably drains you. This is NOT a mood journal. It is a pattern map that grows over time from observed evidence.

The point: over time, this file encodes "when you are good for what" so the morning ritual can match work to energy, not just to calendar availability.

---

## Peak Windows

*When are you sharpest? Morning, afternoon, evening? What type of work fits each window?*

## Depletion Signals

*What reliably drains your energy? Back-to-back meetings? Certain types of work? Environmental factors?*

## Reliable Recovery

*What recharges you? Exercise, walks, naps, specific activities?*

---

## How This File Gets Updated

Energy patterns emerge from observation, not deliberate tracking. As daily notes accumulate evidence ("crushed it this morning" vs. "couldn't focus after lunch"), patterns surface. The weekly review is a natural time to add observations here.
